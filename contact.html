FAQ

Common Questions

What kind of collaborations do you accept?+
I'm open to hotel & resort stays, tourism board campaigns, travel gear reviews, lifestyle product integrations, sponsored Instagram content, YouTube vlogs, and SEO blog features. Everything must genuinely align with my travel audience — I don't promote things I wouldn't personally use or recommend.
How quickly do you respond to collaboration requests?+
WhatsApp messages usually get a reply within 2 hours during the day. Email proposals are typically answered within 24–48 hours. For time-sensitive projects, WhatsApp is always the fastest route.
Do you do barter / gifted collaborations?+
For hotel stays and destination experiences, gifted/hosted collaborations are considered depending on the property and location. For product-only collaborations, I typically work on a paid basis. Send a message and we can discuss what works for both sides.
What is your content focus?+
My primary focus is Northeast India travel — Assam, Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram and Tripura. I also cover pan-India travel occasionally. My content style is minimalist, authentic, and storytelling-first — not flashy or oversaturated.
Can I request a media kit with full stats?+
Yes! Email hello@himangshu.in with subject "Media Kit Request" or drop a WhatsApp message and I'll send across the full media kit with audience demographics, engagement rates, platform stats and collaboration packages.
