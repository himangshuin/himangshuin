Local Tips

Before You Go to Northeast India

Insider advice from a born-and-raised Assamese traveller.

📋
Get Your ILP in Advance
Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Mizoram & Manipur require an Inner Line Permit. Apply online well before your trip — some states take 3–5 days to process.
🌦️
Best Season: Oct–April
Monsoon brings landslides and road closures across hilly Northeast states. The sweet spot is November to February — clear skies, crisp air, and Kaziranga wide open.
🚗
Book Local Transport Early
Shared Sumo/Bolero services are the backbone of Northeast travel. Book seats a day before, especially for popular routes like Guwahati–Shillong.
💰
Carry Cash
ATMs are sparse in remote Northeast destinations. Carry enough cash for at least 3–4 days — especially in Arunachal, parts of Nagaland and hill areas of Meghalaya.
🏠
Choose Local Homestays
Homestays give you authentic Northeast hospitality, home-cooked meals, and local knowledge that no hotel can match. They're cheaper and support the community directly.
📱
BSNL SIM for Remote Areas
Private carriers often lose signal in remote Northeast areas. A BSNL SIM is your best bet for connectivity in places like Tawang, Dzukou, or deep Meghalaya.
