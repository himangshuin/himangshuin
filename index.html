Why Assam Is the Gateway to Northeast India

Assam has always been the entry point to India's most geographically diverse region. With Guwahati serving as the transport hub, every journey to Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, or Nagaland begins here.

The Brahmaputra riverfront, Kamakhya Temple, silk heritage — Assam itself is a destination that demands exploration.

Assam TravelGuwahatiNortheast India
02

Best Time to Visit Assam — A Month-by-Month Guide

October to April is the prime travel window — Kaziranga reopens, weather is crisp, and the landscape is most vibrant. The Bihu festivals bring Assam's culture to life.

The best months are November and February. The fog lifts, rhinos are visible, and the Northeast shimmers.

03

How to Become a Travel Content Creator in Northeast India

Being a content creator in Northeast India comes with unique challenges and extraordinary rewards. The region is one of India's most under-documented — which means if you're creating here, you're often a pioneer.

Start local, be consistent, prioritise your community. The Northeast has stories the world hasn't heard yet.
