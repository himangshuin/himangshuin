Hello, I'm Himangshu 👋

I'm a travel storyteller, content creator & minimalist vlogger based in Guwahati, Assam. I've spent years exploring the extraordinary landscapes, cultures, and stories that make Northeast India one of the most underrated regions in the world — and sharing every single one with my growing community.

My journey started simply: a phone, a backpack, and a deep curiosity about the Northeast that I grew up calling home. What began as casual Instagram posts from weekend trips around Assam eventually grew into himangshu.in — a full content ecosystem spanning travel guides, mini vlogs, short films, and long-form storytelling.

"Simple Living. Big Adventures. That's how I see the Northeast."

— Himangshu Kalita, @himangshu.in

What I Do 🎥

I create authentic, minimalist content that captures the real Northeast India — not the polished tourist brochure version, but the raw, beautiful, soul-stirring version that locals know and love. From the one-horned rhinos of Kaziranga to the floating island of Majuli, from the living root bridges of Meghalaya to the monasteries of Tawang, I document it all.

As one of Assam's most recognised content creators, my work spans Instagram Reels, YouTube mini vlogs, travel blog guides, and brand collaborations with tourism brands, hotels, and lifestyle companies across the Northeast.

Why Northeast India? 🏔️

Most travellers see the Northeast as a distant corner of India, hard to reach and harder to understand. I see it as the most biodiverse, culturally rich, and visually stunning part of our country — and it deserves to be known.

The Brahmaputra at sunrise. The smell of rain on Shillong's pine forests. The sounds of Bihu drums echoing across Guwahati at night. These are the things I want to share with the world. As a born-and-raised Assamese creator, I have a perspective that no outsider can replicate — and I believe that authenticity matters more than production value.

My Journey

The Himangshu.in Story

From a curious traveller to Assam's best blogger — here's the timeline.

2020
First Steps — Local Assam Exploration
Started sharing photos of Guwahati, Kamakhya Temple, and Brahmaputra sunsets on Instagram. Discovered my passion for visual storytelling through the lens of Assamese culture.
2021
Going Deeper into Northeast India
Expanded beyond Assam into Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, and Nagaland. First viral Reel from Kaziranga National Park. Community began growing organically around authentic Northeast travel content.
2022
Launched the Travel Blog
Himangshu.in was born. Started publishing long-form travel guides for destinations across Northeast India. First brand collaboration with a regional hotel property in Assam.
2023
Mini Vlogs Take Off on YouTube
Launched YouTube channel with mini vlog format — quick, intimate, cinematic looks at Northeast India travel. Crossed 2,000 Instagram followers. Featured in local media as a top Assam content creator.
2024–25
Building the Northeast India Storytelling Platform
Nearly 2,800 Instagram followers. 50+ destinations documented. Multiple brand deals. Launched himangshu.in as a full personal brand platform with SEO-optimised travel guides covering all 7 Northeast sister states.

What Drives Me

My Values as a Creator

01
Authenticity Over Aesthetics
I'd rather show a muddy trail at Dzukou Valley than a perfectly curated Maldives shot. The Northeast is raw, real, and that's what makes it special. My content reflects that.
02
Local First, Always
I support local homestays, local guides, local food, and local businesses. When I feature a place, I make sure the community benefits from the attention — not just the tourism industry.
03
Simple Living, Big Adventures
You don't need a massive budget or fancy gear to explore the Northeast. My philosophy is minimalist travel — do more with less, and find magic in the ordinary.
04
Storytelling That Matters
Every place has a story. Every culture deserves to be heard. I believe content about Northeast India should honour its communities — not just treat them as a backdrop.
